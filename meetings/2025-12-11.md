## General Updates

## GenevaERS Annual Review

Mertic will reach out to project leads concerning access to new IBM infrastructure

Mark Bylok expressed interest in leading the COBOL Check project moving forward.  Bylok and Mertic will speak offline regarding workload and next steps.

Mertic reported the proposal to move the Zorse project to the Emeritus stage was approved.
