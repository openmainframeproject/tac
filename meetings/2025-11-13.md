# Notes

## General Updates

## Zowe Annual Review

Sean Grady presented the annual review for Zowe.

## Ztrust Working Group

Rick Troth presented the proposal for the ztrust Working Group.  A vote to approve the proposal will take place offline in LFX.

## TAC Vice Chair Election

Mertic reported the TAC Vice Chair Election would begin in December.

## Adding Community elected representative roles to the TAC

Mertic presented a recommendation to add the ability for the TAC to select up to 2 individuals yearly as Community Representatives with full voting rights.  Discussion ensued, and Mertic volunteered to summarize the proposal and send it to the TAC mailing list.

## Zorse and COBOL Check Updates

Mertic reported that Zorse and COBOL Check are still in need of maintainers.  Discussion ensued, and Elizabeth Joseph and Mark Bylok offered to recruit for COBOL Check.  Mertic suggested archiving the Zorse project, and the TAC representatives agreed.  A vote will take place offline in LFX.
